WHAT IS METHAPHETAMINE?

Methaphetamine is an illegal drug in the form of white, odorless, bitter-tasting crystalline powder and it is also called as meth, chalk, ice, and crystal but among these names this drug is famously known as "Shabu". Its' chemical formula is C10H15N. Its parent drug, amphetamine was used for nasal congestant and inhalers specifically bronchial inhalers. This illegal drugs mostly targets the Central Nervous System. This drug is classified as Schedule II which means it has high chances of abuse when it is prescribed it cannot be refilled anymore.

Here are some side effects of Methaphetamine when it is not used properly:

  • Headache
  • Loss of Appetite
  • Sexually transmitted infection
  • Violent Behaviors
  • Insomnia
  • Depression

Is Methaphetamine prescribed by doctors?

Yes it is prescribed by other doctors but it is only prescribed when in a low dosage to avoid overdose which can lead to drug addiction. This drug is used to cure deficit hyperactivity disorder and other cases but it is rarely prescribed due to some rules and regulations regarding the usage of the said drug.
